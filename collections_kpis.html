Scope. This dashboard tracks six KPIs for the collections function. Roll rates (goals 1–4) measure what share of originated principal remains outstanding at each DPD milestone, on seasoned vintages (at least 180 days past maturity), weighted by origination volume across the trailing six qualifying months. Goal 5 is the current delinquent-status principal balance. Goal 6 is year-to-date recovery dollars on loans whose maturity has fully passed.

Filters applied. Small and non-core partners are excluded (Relay, Balance, MyToolbox, XSQRD, Buildstock, Ply, Harvv/Qisstpay, Inxeption, Melengo). Koverly loans originated after Oct 31, 2025 are excluded as new book.
